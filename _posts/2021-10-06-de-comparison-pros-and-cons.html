GNOME:

  • pros:
    • easy to use (both for normal users and developers)
    • not modded might save eyes ;)
    • macOs similar look (dock + statusbar menus)
    • installed by default on most distros
  • cons:
    • almost not themable

KDE:

  • pros:
    • windows similar look (bottom bar)
    • very customizable (theme store + ability to make diy themes in files)
    • synchronization with phone (requires app)
  • cons: 
    • too much features
    • eats battery
    • requires many memory

 CINNAMON:

  • pros:
    • very customizable
    • light 
    • windows-similar look (bottom bar)
  • cons:
    • a little outdated 

 XFCE:

  • pros:
    • very customizable
    • light
  • cons: 
    • doesnt look good by default :/

 PANTHEON:

  • pros: 
    • macOs similar look (dock + statusbar)
  • cons: 
    • not fully themable (not able to create themes BUT there are a few preinstalled)

 MATE:

  • pros: 
    • light
    • 2 sidebars
    • themable
  • cons: 
    • doesnt look like new one by default...
